Wordhord

Welcome to my wordhord! I was inspired to start one by oldenglishwordhord.com. A wordhord is a collection of nice words and phrases to draw upon, used by poets as they composed verse! For my purposes, this section of my site will serve as a repository of words I like as well as a place for me to express my general love for language! I have always been fascinated by linguistics, philology, ancient languages, puns, poetry, and word play, so expect lots of that.
